Overview

What is Agni Club Register?

Agni Club Register is the official account creation flow for the Agni Club mobile gaming platform. It is the short, friendly process that introduces you to the platform, sets up your profile and creates the credentials you will use for every future Agni Club Login. From the moment registration is complete, your account becomes the carrier of your progress, friends, replays and reward history across every season.

The signup flow is engineered to be welcoming rather than overwhelming. There are no long forms, no unnecessary questions and no pressure to spend money. Agni Club asks only for what is genuinely required to keep your account secure: a mobile number, a password and a quick OTP verification. Once that is done, you are inside the app and ready to play.

Agni Club is mobile-first, so the entire Agni Club Signup experience is optimised for phones. Field labels are clear, the keyboard switches to the correct type automatically and the verification OTP is auto-detected on supported Android devices. Even on a low-bandwidth connection, the whole process typically completes in just a few minutes.

Step-by-step Agni Club registration guide

The full Agni Club Signup flow is intentionally short. The whole process is built to take less than five minutes, even on a slow connection.

  1. Open Agni Club on your phone or visit the official website.
  2. Tap the Register button on the home screen.
  3. Enter your mobile number with the correct country code.
  4. Tap Send OTP and wait for the one-time password to arrive.
  5. Enter the OTP — on supported devices it will auto-fill.
  6. Choose a strong password you do not use anywhere else.
  7. (Optional) Enter an Agni Club Gift Code or invite code from a friend.
  8. Accept the community guidelines and confirm your age.
  9. Tap Create Account. You will be signed in automatically.

Tips for new users

A few small choices during Agni Club Register will make your long-term experience much smoother. None of them take more than a few seconds, and all of them pay off.

Use your real mobile number

Your registered number is how you recover your account if you ever lose your password. A throwaway number means a throwaway account.

Pick a memorable username

You will see it on every match summary and leaderboard. Choose something you are happy to be known by inside the Agni Club community.

Set a long passphrase

Four random words are easier to remember and far stronger than a short complex password.

Personalise your profile early

An avatar and short bio help friends find you and make your account feel like home from day one.

Bookmark the login page

Save the Agni Club Login page so future sign-ins are one tap away.

Explore at your own pace

You do not have to play ranked immediately. Casual lobbies are the perfect warm-up for new players.

Account verification information

Verification is a small but important part of Agni Club Register. It confirms two things: that the mobile number on your account is real, and that it actually belongs to you. Both are vital for keeping the community safe and ensuring that recovery actually works when you need it.

  • Mobile OTP — a one-time password sent to your registered number during signup.
  • Device check — new devices may request an additional confirmation during your first Agni Club Login.
  • Age confirmation — you confirm you meet the legal age requirement in your jurisdiction.
  • Community guidelines — you accept the rules that keep the Agni Club community healthy.

If an OTP does not arrive within a minute, request a resend. Check your signal strength, make sure your inbox is not full, and confirm that your number was entered with the correct country code.

Safe registration tips

The signup screen is the moment where good habits are easiest to set. A few simple choices during Agni Club Signup will protect your account for as long as it exists.

Register from the official app

Always download Agni Club from official channels. Avoid sideloaded clones that imitate the brand.

Never share your OTP

Agni Club support will never ask you for an OTP. Anyone who does is impersonating the brand.

Use a unique password

Do not reuse a password from another service. A breach elsewhere should never compromise Agni Club.

Watch for fake gift codes

Only redeem an Agni Club Gift Code from official Agni Club channels and community announcements.

Keep recovery info updated

If your mobile number changes, update it before you lose access to the old SIM.

Play responsibly

Set healthy session limits from day one. The Responsible Gaming page has helpful guidance.
